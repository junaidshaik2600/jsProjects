Now this is the most important phase of your entire project 🔥
You’re no longer building — you’re validating your product like a real user.

I’ll give you a clear checklist you can literally follow while using your app daily.


---

🧠 HOW TO USE THIS CHECKLIST

Don’t test like a developer.
👉 Use it like your personal life tool.

While using, ask:

> “Does this feel natural or annoying?”




---

✅ 1. DIARY (CORE OF YOUR APP)

📝 Entry Flow

Can I create an entry in under 10 seconds?

Does “Write for today” feel natural?

Are most-used fields at the top?


⚡ Speed

Any lag while typing?

Any delay while saving?

Any UI flicker?


🧱 Structure

Do the 8 sections feel:

logical?

or too much?



🤔 Real Usage Check

Did you skip filling some fields? 👉 If yes → those fields are unnecessary or poorly placed


🔒 Lock System

Lock/unlock works smoothly?

No accidental edits?



---

👥 2. PEOPLE SYSTEM

👀 List View

Can you quickly recognize people?

Duplicate names handled clearly?


🎂 Birthday System

Shows:

today birthdays?

upcoming birthdays?


Age calculation correct?


📊 Detail Page

Meeting count accurate?

Last interaction visible?


🧠 Real Usage

Did you actually open a person’s profile? 👉 If not → UX needs improvement



---

🔍 3. SEARCH & FILTER

⚡ Speed

Filters apply instantly?

No delay?


🎯 Usefulness

Do you actually use filters?

Or ignore them?


🔁 Clear/Reset

Easy to reset?

No confusion?


🧠 Real Check

Can you find an old entry in <5 seconds?



---

📚 4. LIBRARY SYSTEM

📂 Navigation

Is it clear what each section does?

No confusion between:

Diary

Archive

People

Food etc.



🧠 Mental Model

Ask yourself: 👉 “Does this feel like my personal database?”


---

🧭 5. NAVIGATION FLOW

🔁 Movement

Switching tabs feels smooth?

Back button always safe?


❌ Check for:

Getting lost

Too many steps

Confusing paths



---

⚡ 6. PERFORMANCE

🔄 Check:

Scrolling smooth?

No lag on:

lists

editor

search



📱 Test on:

Your main phone

Low-end device (if possible)



---

💥 7. CRASH TEST (VERY IMPORTANT)

Try to break it:

Tap buttons fast

Open/close screens quickly

Cancel mid-action

Switch screens repeatedly


👉 Expected: ❌ No crashes
❌ No freezes


---

🎨 8. UI FEEL (HONEST CHECK)

Ask yourself:

Does this feel:

premium?

or basic?


Is spacing consistent?

Do cards look uniform?

Any ugly or odd-looking screen?



---

🧠 9. DAILY USAGE TRUTH TEST

At the end of the day:

👉 Did you actually use the app naturally?

Or:

Forced yourself to use it?

Avoided certain features?



---

📊 10. WRITE THESE DOWN (CRITICAL)

Keep notes like:

“Too many taps here”

“This field useless”

“This should be faster”

“This is confusing”


👉 These are more valuable than any code


---

🚨 RED FLAGS (IF YOU SEE THESE → FIX LATER)

You avoid opening the app

You skip sections repeatedly

You feel friction every time

You forget to log things



---

🟢 GREEN SIGNS (YOU’RE DOING IT RIGHT)

You open app naturally

You log entries quickly

You enjoy using it

It feels like your own system



---

🚀 AFTER 2–3 DAYS

Come back and tell me:

👉 What felt:

annoying

slow

unnecessary

powerful



---

👉 Phase 2 (Work system + intelligence layer)


---

🧠 FINAL THOUGHT

Right now you’re not testing code.

👉 You’re testing your life system

And this is where your app becomes truly unique.
